Sound

ALSA (Advanced Linux Sound Architecture) is the low-level system (Kernel level)

PULSEAUDIO is a high-level service to mix different sound channels.

for alsa, check aplay, arecord and aplay -l

recording with arecord (10 seconds, stereo, cd quality: 16 bits 44.1khz):

arecord -d 10 -f cd -t wav test.wav

mono, 8 bits, 8khz

arecord -t wav --max-file-time 10 test.wav

for pulseaudio, check pavucontrol, pavumeter

launch command-line sound control panel

alsamixer
