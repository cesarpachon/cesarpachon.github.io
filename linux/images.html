Image manipulation

list all images with their dimension in pixels

feh -l

generate a image with thumbnails of all the images of a folder

feh -m

resize all images in a folder (mogrify to overwrite, convert to save as new)

mogrify -resize 200 *.png

convert all jpg images in png, keeping the originals:

mogrify -format png *.jpg

merge: mix images with alpha channel to produce a single one

convert assets/skin/skin1.png assets/hair/hair0.png assets/face/face1.png -flatten out.png
